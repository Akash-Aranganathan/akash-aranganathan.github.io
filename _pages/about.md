My interest has been to investigate biological molecules. I began my research career with the analysis of protein folding intermediates and pathways during my undergraduate studies at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. In Dr. Athi N. Naganathan's lab, I worked on an Ising-like model called **block-WSME (bWSME)**. Currently, in my Ph.D. I am working with Dr. Pratyush Tiwary in combining Artificial Intelligence and statistical mechanics to develop models for investigating and predicting protein conformational ensembles. To this end, I have developed a methodology, **AlphaFold2RAVE**, to calculate the free energy landscape and a biologically relevant reaction coordinate for any given protein sequence.
